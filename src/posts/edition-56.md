## Where Dreams Come True

“I would just say, it was magical. Everything they did was well done.” -Zachary B. “It was so fun! And there was the log ride, and the teapot ride was so funny!” -Lottie B. “It was very fun.” -Elsie B. This week, as you may have figured out, Blackwood Boarding had the chance to attend the renowned, “Walt Disney World” theme park! The visit was, of course, full of thrills and excitement for everyone. We enjoyed meeting the Disney characters like Mickey Mouse and Cinderellla, as well as riding some of the intense hair-raising roller coasters! The iconic Disney World castle stood prominently at the center of the “Magic Kingdom” which made some great photo ops for Flo Blackwood to take advantage of. Spending two whole days in the park, BBSG was able to get the full Disney experience, with entertaining activities all day long. By the end of the first day, Lottie Blackwood was too exhausted to even stand up in line for the boat ride back to the campground, and fell asleep on the floor instead. As exhibited, Disney World may siphon away your energy, but it’s loads of fun for the whole family, and everyone at BBSG would highly recommend it! 

## Time with Aussie Cousins

Since we were both visiting the southern area, we were able to see our beloved Auntie Maria, Uncle Nathaniel, and cousins Mabel and Lucilla from Australia for the afternoon at a park in Florida. Playing, talking, eating, and of course taking plenty of pictures, everyone enjoyed the time we were able to spend with them. And after hanging out at the park for a few hours, we concluded by going to Chick-fil-a for dinner and then saying farewell. 

## Saturday Touristing

We spent this Saturday in St. Augustine, the oldest surviving city in America, and witnessed many historical sights. Walking along the two oldest streets in the U.S. we also saw the oldest wooden school house in the country. Lots of old stuff. Meandering around town, we stopped to observe a fort, and looked around the pirate museum which was very interesting and informative. There is lots of history in this city that we enjoyed to see and hear about.
