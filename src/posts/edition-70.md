## A Fun Holiday at the Lake

Last Monday, as you may have realized, was Memorial Day. BBSG traveled an hour and a half to Aunt Lara and Uncle Robert’s house to swim in their pond, tube on the lake, play egg-toss, and of course see extended family. “I loved the swimming and trampoline. I liked tubing, and yeah. I only fell off once.” said student Elsie Blackwood. After a full day of outdoor activities, the Blackwood Boarding Girls returned home with plenty of sunburns and fun memories. 

## Finishing Touches on The Manor

This week the builders of Blackwood Manor have been hard at work finishing up their last projects and even hanging up photographs and paintings on the walls. Flo Blackwood thinks, “it is really feeling more and more like home!” Even though the interior of the house is nearly finished, the outside still has plenty of work to be done, like the painting, back porch, and front door. But, some new developments inside like the bathtub, light fixtures, and stair railings are really bringing the space together. BBSG’s hopeful move-in date is now Saturday, June sixth!

## RGFC Coming up Soon

“I can’t wait for church camp! Eeee!” said Emory B. in an interview about the upcoming Redeeming Grace Family Camp, or RGFC. Everyone at Blackwood Boarding is ecstatic to attend this year, and as Emory put it, can’t wait. This year, everyone is especially excited for a new activity, the greased watermelon race! Student Allison Blackwood exclaimed, “I’m so excited!” This year will expectedly be loads of fun with delightful classes, entertaining games, and great friends.
