## Ag-tastic Burger Bash

The annual Purdue University “Ag Week” was held on campus this past week, with a different event every day. Blackwood Boarding School took a field trip there on the “Burger Bash” day along with the Lutz family. But bashers didn’t just wait in line being bored for an hour and a half for free cheeseburgers. All through the line there were people behind tables giving out agricultural freebies! There were squishy foam pigs, corn stickers, potted flowers, sunglasses, highlighters, baseball caps, chapstick, and a lot of other stuff (some of which may not have had much to do with agriculture.) But students enjoyed collecting the giveaways, and relished the burgers at the end!

## Let the Games Begin

Well both Elsie and Felicity Blackwood faced their first soccer games this week, and they didn’t actually go that terribly! Elsie Blackwood stated, “We did pretty well, but we definitely could’ve done a lot better.” Her team, the Jello Wellos won with three to one, and the Black Tei-dyes (Felicity’s team) creamed their opponents with five to one. “I wasn’t as bad as I expected.” said Felicity, “But thankfully our other teammates know what they're doing.”
