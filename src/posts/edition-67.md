## Moving Partay!

As construction on Blackwood Manor finishes up, and the big move-in day is drawing near, what was sitting in storage rooms a week ago is finally inside the house. BBSG hosted our first party in the new building when over 50 people came to help move boxes and furniture in, eat pizza, and celebrate Flo Blackwood’s 37th birthday with cookies and ice cream! While the young children had a blast producing complete chaos in the basement, the strong and able hauled items out of trucks and into our soon-to-be living space. The moving bash was a huge help getting everything in and set up, and Blackwood Boarding gives a big thanks to everyone who came to lend some muscle!! …Now it’s time to unpack. 

## Adventures in Wonderland

This week were the successful performances of Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland by the Christus Homines Drama Troupe, of which two BBSG students are participants. Felicity Blackwood played the lively role of the Red Chess Queen, and Elsie Blackwood acted as Lily, the talking flower. Flo B. remarked, “It was a really fun \[play]! The costumes were very colorful!” And of course she made sure to have plenty of photo and video evidence to prove it! Principal Zachary commented, “The performance was the best yet!” Both Felicity and Elsie enjoyed being in this year’s play through countless rehearsals, tech week, and finally    breaking their legs on stage.  

## A Not-So-Tiny Celebration

“The best part was having lots of friends.” said Emory Blackwood after her official 10th birthday party at Blackwood Manor. She and her friends loved playing games and eating tons of cake (a slightly larger dessert than the cake Emory made on her real birthday.) Unfortunately this one split right down the middle when Emroy placed a fondant cherry in the middle of the sprinkle-covered confection. But, she remarked, “It was still fun even when the cake fell apart. I liked all of it.”
