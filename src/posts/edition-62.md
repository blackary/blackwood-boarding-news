## Fluffy New Friends at BBSG

Last Tuesday, when some of Blackwood Boarding was killing time out of the house at Rural King, we decided to pick up a little more than free popcorn. We returned, marching into the house triumphantly, carrying a chirping cardboard box with holes in it. “Chickies!” Lottie Blackwood cried with surprise as she opened the container. Since we were already planning to obtain a small flock of chickens for Blackwood Manor, it was easy to convince Mom to go ahead and buy them now, since they won’t be for sale by the time we move in. The feathery little hatchlings have given students something to do when not “killing time out of the house” especially Allison and Lottie Blackwood. In alphabetical order, the chickens’ names are: Berry, Cherry, Chip (R.I.P.), Froda, Gem, Laya, Lilac (R.I.P.), Omelet, Pippa, and Tamika. Principal Zachary said about his chick, (Yes even he got one!) “She has hairy feet so she needed a Hobbit name, but Frodo is a boy so it had to be Froda.” Allison B. said about her chick, “I love to pet and snuggle with mine. And it’s name is Princess Laya from Star Wars, and like Lay an egg!” Everyone at BBSG is obviously loving the fluffy new editions to the school.

## A Colorful Easter

Easter egg decorating and hunting is always a fun tradition around this time of year when we celebrate the resurrection of Jesus (although what do eggs have to do with that???) So Blackwood Boarding Girls had fun with a small egg hunt, and painting and dying a few eggs…and maybe some hair too. If you have never seen green bangs before, just drop by BBG sometime. Happy Easter!  Let the Soccer Games Begin!
