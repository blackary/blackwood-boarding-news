## Unpacking 

After the big moving party at Blackwood Manor, Flo B. and others from BBSG have been busy unpacking all the boxes and organizing the house. Flo exclaimed, “Oh my word, it’s going so good! The kitchen is unpacked, most of the bedrooms are unpacked…we’re making great progress.” While Lottie Blackwood stated, “I love unpacking! Since I get to see things that I forgot about and then I’m like ‘Yaaayyy!’ And then it’s just like my birthday!” It’s obviously going well, but there’s still a little ways to go before moving in. 

## Farewell to the Soccer Season

This week Felicity and Elsie played their final soccer games of the season. Felicity’s team, the black tie dyes, lost the tournament to the pink team in the final four, but they played their best. Felicity commented, “Well, at least we made it to the final four, though, there were only four teams in the league, so it was actually our first game in the tournament… But it was still pretty good even though we lost.” It was a great season, but a brutal loss at the last game. Elsie, on the other hand, won her last game by a landslide! She also impressively kicked the assist to the assist to the winning goal! It was a good season for both teams. At least, they all survived anyway. 

## The Fish are Alive!

Last summer, principal Zachary Blackwood dumped bags of baby fish into the pond, but sadly, they seemed to have all died over the winter. However, just a few days ago, two fish were miraculously caught when some students were testing if any of last summers’ fish were left. The test-run fishers caught plenty of seaweed, algae, and sticks, before finally getting live, scally, slimy fish. Lottie Blackwood stated about the phenomenon, “We were just fishing at our pond. And the fish are alive!” Hopefully we will have lots of fun reeling in more this summer!
