## Pedaling Away

This week we’ve gotten our old bicycles out of storage and have been riding away. Lottie Blackwood is now learning more how to bike with the pedals instead of just pushing off the ground. She has really made a lot of progress over the past few days as it was getting warmer and everyone was spending more time outside. Unfortunately, just when you’d think we’re headed for spring, the weather has suddenly taken a wintery turn with lots of wind, cold, and snow! This hasn’t made the best bike-practicing environment, so Lottie and the rest of BBSGirls have had to stick to indoor activities, but we are hopeful that we’ll be pedaling away again soon! 

## Leisurely Weekend Activities

This Saturday most of BBSG had the chance to attend The Magical Elephant play to watch one of our Faris cousins act as the head of an elephant for a high school performance in Indianapolis. There were mixed feelings about the production however, as Elsie Blackwood simply remarked, “Eh, it was fine.”  while on the other hand, Zachary Blackwood stated, “I was thoroughly impressed! The quality of the singing and the choreography was very high.” While the play was transpiring, Felicity B. was on a birthday girls’ shopping spree for her friend Jane Lutz, who some of you may recall from earlier issues back in May. They also had a magnificent time strolling through the mall and eating a delectable lunch at Chick-fil-a. 

## Hand-me-downs Boost Clothing Economy 

Since the RV trip, BBSG has been stuck with very few clothing options, as we haven't gotten much out of storage. Also many of our outfits that we wore over and over again in the RV are worn out or have gotten too small. But it was hand-me-downs to the rescue this week for some of the older students, when we were given two giant trashbags bursting with garments. After going through the scarves, sweaters, dresses and other wardrobe items Elsie B. mentioned, “It was perfect timing.” And Flo Blackwood, after witnessing the vast heaps of clothing remarked, “Wow! Your wardrobe has quadrupled in size! Who needs to go to the store?”
