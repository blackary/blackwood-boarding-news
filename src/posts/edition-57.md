## RV Party Joins Roommate Reunion

Grandma Faris who was visiting her old college roommate and maid of honor in North Carolina was having a wonderful time reuniting when all of the sudden, Blackwood Boarding School for Girls pulled into the driveway! We marched in, ready to devour a promised batch of beignets (Not beige nets, bin yays. It’s French.) and play with some of the stuffed animals upstairs. After finishing off the chocolate-covered deserts, we drove away, taking G’ma with us! While some of these events may have been slightly dramatized, the fun was not. 

## Back Home Again in Indiana…To Stay! 

This week, Classy, or the Repulsively Vitriolic, trundled home for the last time. Blackwood Boarding Girls leaped out of the vehicle, singing and dancing for joy! One anonymous member of the Blackwood Boarding News staff commented, “Well, it was sometimes fun while it lasted, but it is SO nice to be back for good! We have so much space, we can run around inside and I can actually sit up in my room without crouching down or bumping my head! And it’s also great to be able to see all our friends again in person! (postcards were really getting old)” Flo Blackwood stated, “Well, there’s a lot of mixed emotions. Bitter-sweet for sure. I am SO glad we did it, and I highly recommend it to anybody. But, I am so glad to be back home, and I am loving to see the new house frequently.” 

## RV For Sale

Since Classy’s time with us has come to a close, she’s up for sale and ready to go. After everyone moved in all the things and did some cleaning, Flo Blackwood deep-cleaned the RV inside and out,  and declared that, “No one is allowed in except serious potential buyers!” If anyone is interested in purchasing a slightly used camper, contact BBSG and if you're lucky, you may have a chance at making it onto the “people allowed inside the RV” list!
